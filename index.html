Requirement discovery & operational audit

We analyze how your communication operations function today and identify where automation can deliver the most impact.

  • Interaction types
  • Communication volumes
  • Reasons for contact
  • Agent workflows and SOPs
  • Escalation paths
  • Current system stack

We define what should be automated — and what should not.

KNOWLEDGE

Knowledge structuring

Operational knowledge is structured into deterministic systems so automation behaves predictably and safely.

  • Scripts and SOPs
  • Policy documents
  • FAQs
  • Historical interaction data
  • Existing decision rules

Knowledge is structured deterministically — not left to uncontrolled generation.

  • Test edge cases and escalation behaviour
  • Refine flows using operational feedback

Systems are validated for predictability and safety — not just conversational accuracy.
